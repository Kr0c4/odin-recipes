Ingredients

  • some meat
  • and a bit of a veggie
  • salt, pepper (red and black too)
  • water
  • your freetime, because you won't be ready in a hour

Steps

  1. wash everything and cut them
  2. put everything in a pot, it should be a bit bigger than a mug
  3. start the stove and get a spoon, you will need it
  4. wait till it boils, than turn it down to medium strength
  5. after 2 and a half - 3 hours it is ready
