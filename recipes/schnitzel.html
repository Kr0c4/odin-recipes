Ingredients

  • meat of your choice
  • eggs
  • flour
  • breadcrumb
  • potato or rice

Steps

  1. cut the meat into thin slices, should be around 5 mm
  2. put the slices in eggs, than in flour, than in eggs again and last in breadcrumbs
  3. after you've done it to all slices, bake them in the oven or in a pan in some oil
  4. make some side dish of your choice, like smashed potatos, fries, rice ...
  5. some spicy sauce makes it even better
